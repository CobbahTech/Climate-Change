This website presents my research on the impact of climate change on small-scale farmers in South Africa.

The Problem

Small-scale farmers in South Africa are facing serious challenges due to climate change. Changes in rainfall patterns, prolonged droughts, and rising temperatures are disrupting farming activities.

Limited access to irrigation, funding, and modern technology makes adaptation difficult, leading to food insecurity and income loss.

Research Findings

Primary Research

Unpredictable weather, drought, and reduced yields reported by farmers.

Secondary Research

Climate change is increasing temperatures and variability, affecting farmers severely.
